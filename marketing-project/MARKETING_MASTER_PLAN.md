# GHAWDEX SOLAR - MASTER MARKETING PLAN

**Project:** Ghawdex Solar Installation Company - Marketing Campaign
**Location:** Malta
**Target Market:** Maltese homeowners and businesses interested in solar energy
**Campaign Start:** 2025
**Managed by:** Marketing Agent (Claude)

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This marketing plan is structured in **4 distinct phases** to systematically build brand awareness, create compelling content, launch targeted ad campaigns, and execute/optimize marketing efforts for Ghawdex Solar's 14-day solar installation service in Malta.

**Core Brand Promise:** Professional solar installation in 14 days with real-time building analysis technology.
