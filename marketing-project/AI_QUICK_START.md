## üí° PRO TIPS

### HeyGen Tips
- **Voice Selection:** Test 2-3 voices, use the most natural
- **Avatar Choice:** Professional appearance = more trust
- **Script Length:** Keep under 90 seconds (attention span)
- **Backgrounds:** Choose clean, professional, or Malta-themed

### Midjourney Tips
- **Malta-Specific:** Always include "Malta", "Maltese architecture", "Mediterranean"
- **Photorealistic:** Add "photorealistic" to prompts for ads
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Instagram feed: --ar 1:1
  - Instagram Story: --ar 9:16
  - Facebook ad: --ar 1:1 or --ar 4:5
  - Website hero: --ar 16:9
- **Iterations:** Generate 4 versions, pick best, upscale

### Claude/ChatGPT Tips
- **Be Specific:** More context = better output
- **Iterate:** If output isn't perfect, ask for revisions
- **Humanize:** Edit AI content to sound natural
- **Fact-Check:** Verify any statistics or claims
- **Use Claude for:** Long-form (blog posts, guides, strategy)
- **Use ChatGPT for:** Quick tasks (captions, headlines, scripts)
