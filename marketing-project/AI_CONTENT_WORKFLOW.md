## üí° PRO TIPS FOR AI CONTENT

### HeyGen Best Practices
‚úÖ **Do:**
- Use for explainer videos, company intros, quick tips
- Choose avatars that match your brand (professional, trustworthy)
- Keep scripts under 90 seconds (attention span)
- Generate multiple versions (A/B test different avatars)
- Add captions in post-production (accessibility + sound-off viewing)

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Use for highly emotional content (real faces work better)
- Over-rely on AI avatars (mix with real team videos when possible)
- Use generic backgrounds (customize with Malta imagery)

### Midjourney Best Practices
‚úÖ **Do:**
- Be specific: "Maltese limestone house" not just "house"
- Use aspect ratios: --ar 16:9 (horizontal), --ar 9:16 (vertical), --ar 1:1 (square)
- Request photorealistic style for ads (more authentic)
- Generate 4+ variations per prompt (iterate)
- Upscale final selections for high quality

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Use AI images of people's faces in ads (can look uncanny, fails Meta review sometimes)
- Use without editing (add text/branding in Canva)
- Forget to test different prompts (iterate for best results)

### Claude/ChatGPT Best Practices
‚úÖ **Do:**
- Provide context (audience, tone, goal)
- Give examples of good content
- Request multiple variations (A/B test)
- Edit AI output (make it sound human)
- Use Claude for long-form (blog posts, guides)
- Use ChatGPT for quick tasks (captions, headlines)

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Copy-paste without editing (always humanize)
- Use vague prompts (be specific)
- Forget to fact-check (especially statistics)
- Over-rely on AI voice (inject your brand personality)
