## PERSONA 1: "Energy-Conscious Emma"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 35-45
- **Location:** Mellieha, St. Paul's Bay, or Northern Malta (newer residential areas)
- **Occupation:** Professional (teacher, nurse, office manager)
- **Income:** €30,000-45,000/year (household income €50,000-70,000)
- **Family:** Married with 2 children (ages 5-12)
- **Home:** Owns 3-bedroom house with good roof access
- **Education:** University degree

### Psychographics
- **Values:** Sustainability, family security, long-term planning
- **Lifestyle:** Busy working parent, value-conscious but willing to invest in quality
- **Tech Savvy:** Moderate - uses Facebook daily, reads blogs, trusts Google reviews
- **Environmental Awareness:** High - concerned about climate change and teaching children good values

### Goals & Motivations
- Reduce monthly electricity bills (currently €150-250/month)
- Make environmentally responsible choices
- Increase home value
- Set good example for children
- Gain energy independence
- Take advantage of government incentives while available

### Pain Points & Fears
- **Overwhelmed by options:** "There are so many solar companies, how do I choose?"
- **Fear of hidden costs:** "What if the price doubles after I commit?"
- **Installation disruption:** "I don't have time for a 2-month project"
- **Technical complexity:** "I don't understand kW, inverters, panels..."
- **Aesthetic concerns:** "Will it make my house look ugly?"
- **Trust issues:** "What if they damage my roof or do poor work?"

### Buying Journey
1. **Awareness:** Sees high electricity bill, friend mentions solar
2. **Research:** Googles "solar panels Malta cost", reads blogs
3. **Consideration:** Compares 3-4 companies, reads reviews
4. **Decision:** Chooses based on trust, speed, and transparency
5. **Post-Purchase:** Shares experience on Facebook, recommends to friends

### Where to Reach Her
- **Social Media:** Facebook (daily), Instagram (occasionally)
- **Search:** Google ("solar installation Malta", "solar panel cost")
- **Content:** Blog posts, comparison articles, customer testimonials
- **Advertising:** Facebook Ads, Google Search Ads
- **Best Time:** Evenings (7-9pm) and Sunday mornings

### Messaging That Resonates
✅ "Solar installation in 14 days - faster than anyone else in Malta"
✅ "See exactly how much you'll save with real-time building analysis"
✅ "500+ happy families already saving €100+/month"
✅ "Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, 100% installation guarantee"
✅ "Professional team, premium equipment, comprehensive warranty"

❌ Avoid: Technical jargon, pushy sales language, vague promises

### Content Preferences
- Customer testimonial videos (families like hers)
- Before/after cost comparisons
- "Day in the life" installation timeline
- Educational content: "Solar 101 for Malta homeowners"
- Case studies from her area

### Key Decision Factors (Ranked)
1. **Trust & Reviews** (4.8+ stars essential)
2. **Speed of Installation** (14 days is compelling)
3. **Total Cost & ROI** (wants 5-7 year payback)
4. **Warranty & Support** (peace of mind)
5. **Aesthetic Quality** (must look professional)
