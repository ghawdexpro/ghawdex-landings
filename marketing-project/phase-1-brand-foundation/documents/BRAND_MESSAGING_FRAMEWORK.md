## BRAND VOICE & TONE

### Brand Personality Traits
- **Professional but approachable** (not stuffy or overly technical)
- **Confident but not arrogant** (we know our stuff, but we're humble)
- **Innovative but practical** (cutting-edge tech, real-world results)
- **Energetic but not pushy** (enthusiastic about solar, not aggressive sales)
- **Clear but not condescending** (simple language, not dumbed down)

### Writing Style Guidelines

**DO:**
‚úÖ Use active voice: "We install your solar system in 14 days"
‚úÖ Lead with benefits: "Save ‚Ç¨150/month" before "10kW system"
‚úÖ Use specific numbers: "500+ installations" not "many installations"
‚úÖ Write conversationally: "You'll save money" not "Savings will be realized"
‚úÖ Keep sentences short: Aim for 15-20 words average
‚úÖ Use "you/your" frequently: Customer-focused language

**DON'T:**
‚ùå Use jargon without explanation: Never "kWh" without context
‚ùå Make vague claims: "Best in Malta" without proof
‚ùå Use all caps or excessive exclamation marks!!!
‚ùå Be negative about competitors: Focus on our strengths
‚ùå Overwhelm with technical details: Save for FAQs
