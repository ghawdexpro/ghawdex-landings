## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC BEST PRACTICES

### Facebook
- **Best times:** Weekdays 1-4pm, Saturday 12-1pm
- **Post frequency:** 1-2x/day
- **Optimal length:** 40-80 characters (higher engagement)
- **Visual:** Always include image/video
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 relevant hashtags

### Instagram
- **Best times:** Weekdays 11am-1pm, 7-9pm
- **Post frequency:** 1-2x/day (Feed), 3-5x/day (Stories)
- **Optimal hashtags:** 5-10 relevant (up to 30 allowed)
- **Visual:** High-quality, on-brand imagery
- **Captions:** Mix of short (1 line) and long (storytelling)

### LinkedIn
- **Best times:** Weekdays 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm
- **Post frequency:** 2-3x/week
- **Optimal length:** 150-300 characters (shorter = better)
- **Content:** Professional, value-driven, thought leadership
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 industry-relevant

### Email
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am or 2pm
- **Frequency:** Weekly newsletter, automated drip campaigns
- **Subject line:** 40-50 characters, clear benefit
- **Preview text:** Optimize (don't repeat subject)
- **Content:** Scannable, clear CTA, mobile-optimized

## PERFORMANCE TRACKING

### Key Metrics by Platform

**Facebook:**
- Reach (how many people saw post)
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares / reach)
- Click-through rate (link clicks / impressions)
- Top-performing post types

**Instagram:**
- Impressions
- Engagement rate
- Saves (high intent indicator)
- Story completion rate
- Profile visits

**Blog/Website:**
- Page views
- Time on page
- Bounce rate
- Conversions (form fills)

**Email:**
- Open rate (aim: >20%)
- Click rate (aim: >3%)
- Conversion rate
- Unsubscribe rate (<0.5%)

### Monthly Reporting Template
- Total content published (by type)
- Top 5 performing posts (by engagement)
- Total reach/impressions

- Leads generated from content
- Lessons learned / insights
