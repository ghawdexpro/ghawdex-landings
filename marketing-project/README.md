## ğŸ’¡ GUIDING PRINCIPLES

### 1. Phase-Based Approach
**Don't skip phases.** Each phase builds on the previous. Jumping ahead leads to gaps.

### 2. Test, Learn, Optimize
**Nothing is perfect on day one.** Expect to iterate. A/B test everything.

### 3. Data-Driven Decisions
**Let data guide you,** not assumptions. Track everything, analyze regularly.

### 4. Customer-Centric Messaging
**Always ask:** "What's in it for the customer?" Lead with benefits, not features.

### 5. Consistency Over Perfection
**Better to post consistently** (even if imperfect) than wait for perfection and post rarely.

### 6. Speed is a Differentiator
**The 14-day installation promise is your superpower.** Lead with it in all messaging.

### 7. Local Focus
**You're Malta's solar expert.** Use local language, local imagery, local social proof.
